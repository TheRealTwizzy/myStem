# Accessibility and Content Sensitivity

## Accessibility is an MVP requirement

Accessibility is not post-launch polish.

## Input

- large touch targets;
- stepped controls;
- tap alternatives to swipe;
- no precision-only sliders;
- keyboard support where platform permits;
- screen-reader operability;
- no required long-press;
- no time-critical dexterity challenge.

## Visual

- scalable text;
- strong contrast;
- icon plus text plus shape for state;
- no color-only communication;
- reduced motion;
- reduced flashing;
- zoom for archive images;
- clear focus order;
- portrait-first and tablet-responsive layouts.

## Audio

- captions or text equivalents for all creature sounds;
- no sound-only critical signal;
- separate volume controls where feasible;
- vibration not required for understanding.

## Cognitive accessibility

- concise observational copy;
- optional simple-language mode;
- consistent terminology;
- progressive disclosure;
- persistent confirmation summaries;
- no hidden irreversible swipe gesture;
- tutorial replay from Knowledge Base.

## Mutation Consent

Each choice must be readable without animation.

The interface must explain broad action and uncertainty without presenting one choice as morally correct.

## Biological warnings

Stable, Strained, Unstable, Decaying, and Recovery must each have:

- text label;
- icon;
- posture or movement cue;
- optional color;
- accessible description.

## Content sensitivity ceiling

The product may depict unsettling human-derived anatomy but must not depict:

- blood;
- gore;
- open wounds;
- traumatic amputation;
- sexualized anatomy;
- explicit predation;
- torture;
- cruelty for shock;
- realistic medical emergency framing.

## Regression sensitivity

Regression is represented as resorption, settling, folding, or biological release. Copy must not say the player caused amputation or mutilation.

## Release sensitivity

Release is permanent and emotionally clear.

The confirmation should be sincere but not manipulative. It must state:

- the Stem will disappear;
- it cannot return;
- no later sighting or recall exists;
- Field Entries remain.

## Monetization sensitivity

- no paywall during the first attachment beat;
- weekly billing clearly disclosed;
- no false scarcity;
- no randomized paid outcome;
- no emotional threat that subscription is required to keep a Stem alive;
- no premium protection from regression.

## Minors and privacy

- collect minimal personal data;
- no visible email sharing;
- no unrestricted chat;
- safe account identifiers;
- parental and regional compliance review before launch;
- moderation hooks for public profile elements.

## AI image safety

Validation rejects any image exceeding the content ceiling.

Failed validation must restore allowance without blaming the user.

## Testing

Accessibility acceptance must include:

- screen-reader walkthrough;
- dynamic text;
- reduced motion;
- color-vision simulation;
- keyboard navigation where applicable;
- one-handed use;
- low-bandwidth state;
- offline read-only state.
