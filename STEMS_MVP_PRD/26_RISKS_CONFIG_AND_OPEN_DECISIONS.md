# Risks, Configuration, and Open Decisions

## Major risks

### Scope

The MVP includes cultivation, procedural biology, trading, gifting, synthesis, subscription, AI illustration, archive, and release.

Mitigation:

- shared primitives;
- strict non-goals;
- one subscription tier;
- no currency economy;
- no combat;
- one-for-one transfers;
- fixed Hollow count.

### AI cost

Three allowances per successful weekly payment create ongoing cost.

Mitigation:

- cap nine;
- no grace generation;
- pre-validation;
- no rerolls;
- approved pose families;
- provider abstraction;
- cost telemetry.

### Player trust

Permanent premium assets and consumed lineage rights create high trust requirements.

Mitigation:

- atomic transactions;
- idempotency;
- provenance;
- clear confirmations;
- asset durability;
- allowance restoration.

### Biological opacity

Hidden systems may feel arbitrary.

Mitigation:

- causal biofeedback;
- consistent history;
- multimodal signals;
- fixtures;
- no decorative contradiction.

### Content combinatorics

148 components can create invalid combinations.

Mitigation:

- anatomy graph;
- prerequisites;
- incompatibilities;
- support auto-inclusion;
- viability validator;
- property-based tests.

### Tone drift

Generated content may become gory, animal-like, cute-mascot, or species-based.

Mitigation:

- hard visual laws;
- terminology linting;
- AI validation;
- human content review;
- golden examples.

## Tunable configuration

Values that may change without rewriting product laws:

- band thresholds by sub-biome;
- meaningful exposure duration;
- Becoming pressure accumulation;
- stage pacing within target ranges;
- sustainment severity timing;
- recovery windows;
- regression warning duration;
- trade listing duration;
- gift expiration;
- AI attempt budget;
- archive thumbnail sizes;
- analytics sampling.

## Non-tunable invariants without product review

- three Hollow slots;
- one living Stem per Hollow;
- fixed spawn anatomy;
- no living-Stem transfer;
- no direct anatomy selection;
- one Field Entry per resolved Becoming;
- one Genetic Entry per Field Entry;
- no premium biology advantage;
- one weekly tier;
- three allowances per successful payment;
- cap nine;
- no rerolls;
- permanent release;
- no ordinary-care death;
- The Choosing occurs once.

## Open decisions

These do not block the canonical package but require implementation decisions:

1. Exact player-facing five-band labels for all 21 unique variables.
2. Final weekly price by platform and region.
3. Final anonymous/public account identifier design.
4. Trade listing expiration duration.
5. Gift offer expiration duration.
6. Exact archive ownership-history visibility.
7. Premium image cache behavior after entitlement lapse.
8. Provider and retry policy for illustration generation.
9. Final moderation process for public profile elements.
10. Notification cadence and quiet-hour defaults.
11. Exact legal retention period for non-player-facing release audit records.
12. Whether Strained Stems may always be released or only below a configured severity threshold.

## Decision protocol

For each open decision record:

- owner;
- deadline;
- options;
- affected documents;
- migration risk;
- test impact;
- analytics impact;
- final decision;
- date.

## Configuration safety

Remote configuration must not:

- grant hidden premium biological advantage;
- violate allowance accounting;
- alter fixed anatomy;
- create new Hollow slots;
- bypass confirmations;
- permit offline permanent changes;
- turn a non-gory asset category into graphic content.
