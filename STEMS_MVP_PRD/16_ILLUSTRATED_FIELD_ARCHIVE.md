# Illustrated Field Archive

## Purpose

The Illustrated Field Archive preserves validated Premium illustrations as durable research records.

## Access

Archive access requires:

- active paid entitlement; or
- platform-confirmed grace.

Inactive/free players retain ownership of Premium Field Entries but cannot access full archive viewing or download.

## Eligible records

Only validated Premium Field Entries enter the archive.

Free sketches do not enter the illustrated archive.

## Archive behavior

The archive is:

- view-only;
- provenance-backed;
- searchable and filterable;
- full-resolution;
- ownership-aware;
- separate from living-Stem state.

Archive access does not grant:

- Field Entry ownership;
- Genetic Entry rights;
- synthesis rights without ownership;
- transfer rights.

## Required record data

- Premium Field Entry ID
- current owner
- original creation date
- illustration creation date
- lifecycle stage
- Becoming ordinal
- The Choosing marker
- Consent response
- biome and sub-biome
- approved pose family
- image asset version
- validation result
- provenance chain
- download metadata

## Download

Paid and grace users may download full-resolution images.

Downloads must include embedded or accompanying provenance sufficient to identify:

- product;
- Field Entry;
- illustration version;
- generation date;
- ownership-independent archive identity.

Do not burn private player data into the visible image.

## Ownership transfer

When a Premium Field Entry is traded or gifted:

- the image remains attached;
- current ownership updates atomically;
- archive provenance appends the transfer;
- old owner loses ownership-based access to that record unless another archive-view rule explicitly permits historical viewing.

The archive itself does not create a second transferable copy.

## Release interaction

Releasing a living Stem does not delete existing archive records or Field Entries.

## Asset durability

Ordinary gameplay must never delete or replace a validated Premium asset.

Changes require:

- explicit corruption recovery;
- versioned migration;
- audit record;
- preserved original when legally and technically possible.

## Failure states

A failed generation:

- creates no archive record;
- attaches no image;
- restores allowance;
- leaves Field Entry Free.

## Search and filters

Recommended MVP filters:

- stage;
- biome family;
- sub-biome;
- Consent response;
- The Choosing;
- Genetic Entry state;
- creation date;
- ownership source.

No rarity filter.

## Accessibility

Archive images require:

- concise alt text derived from validated anatomy;
- zoom;
- screen-reader labels;
- downloadable text metadata;
- no essential information available only inside the image.

## Integrity gates

Launch blocks include:

- wrong image attached to Field Entry;
- asset missing after successful generation;
- archive record without validated image;
- image accessible without required entitlement;
- download provenance mismatch;
- transfer leaving archive ownership inconsistent.
