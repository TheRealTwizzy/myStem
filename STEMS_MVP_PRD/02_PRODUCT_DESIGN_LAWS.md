# Product Design Laws

These laws override feature convenience, monetization opportunity, content volume, and implementation shortcuts.

## Law 1 — One organism is a history, not a species

A Stem is a category of one. The simulation may create convergent anatomy, but it must never assign a fixed species identity, rarity tier, collectible class, or predetermined final form.

## Law 2 — Influence without direct selection

Players manipulate environmental conditions and choose a consent response. They never select a wing, jaw, eye count, body plan, mutation recipe, or final silhouette.

## Law 3 — Human biology remains the root

Every structure must derive from recognizably human anatomy, tissue, physiology, or developmental transformation. Function may resemble swimming, gliding, burrowing, filtering, climbing, or anchoring, but the body must not acquire animal anatomy.

Forbidden examples:

- feathers;
- hooves;
- conventional fish fins;
- insect exoskeletons;
- canine muzzles;
- cat ears;
- reptile scales used as literal animal mimicry.

## Law 4 — Attachment before unease

The initial experience must be genuinely tender and readable. Wrongness accumulates gradually. The player should care before the body becomes unsettling.

## Law 5 — Cozy with a wrong note

Target emotion: wonder-tinged dread plus deepening attachment.

Allowed:

- asymmetry;
- extra human-derived sensory structures;
- bone architecture;
- translucent tissue;
- uncanny posture;
- residual anatomy;
- appetite-coded warmth-seeking.

Forbidden:

- blood;
- gore;
- open wounds;
- traumatic dismemberment;
- sexualized body horror;
- cruelty for shock;
- explicit predation.

## Law 6 — Biology has consequences

Every meaningful adaptation creates support requirements, compromises, dependency, redirected pressure, or future sustainment demand. Mutations may solve one problem while making another harder.

## Law 7 — No hidden pay-to-win

Payment must not:

- improve mutation quality;
- reveal hidden values;
- accelerate lifecycle pacing;
- increase Hollow control;
- guarantee desirable anatomy;
- remove Mutation Debt;
- protect against regression;
- grant more living-Stem capacity.

## Law 8 — Permanent assets must be trustworthy

A validated Premium illustration is generated once, attached permanently to one Field Entry, preserved across transfer, and never subject to reroll. Failure must restore the allowance and leave the card unchanged.

## Law 9 — Irreversible actions require clarity

Synthesis, trade, gift, and release require explicit confirmation. Tutorials may be disabled; irreversible confirmations may not.

## Law 10 — No silent biological catastrophe

Regression cannot occur silently while the player is absent. Ordinary cultivation cannot permanently kill a Stem. Biological decline must be signaled through multiple channels and offer a recoverable path before regression.

## Law 11 — Hidden does not mean arbitrary

Players must not see formulas, probabilities, exact thresholds, recipes, or Mutation Debt values. However, outcomes must remain causally grounded in current conditions, history, anatomy, lineage influence, and prior intervention.

## Law 12 — Biofeedback must be multimodal

No essential state may be communicated by color, sound, animation, or text alone. Posture, movement, rhythm, tissue behavior, environmental interaction, sound, and concise observation copy should reinforce the same underlying cause.

## Law 13 — Free play must remain complete

Free players can create New Seeds, cultivate through all life stages, care for adults, generate Field Entries, trade, gift, and release. Premium provides illustration, archive access, and lineage continuation.

## Law 14 — Social systems exchange records, not living beings

The social economy operates through Field Entries. Living Stems cannot be traded, gifted, stored, or moved between players.

## Law 15 — The body must remember

Later anatomy must build on, compensate for, redirect, or visibly retain earlier structures. Perfect replacement and clean resets are exceptional. Residual earlier-stage anatomy should remain visible.

## Law 16 — The product must remain legible without becoming solvable

Players should understand broad needs and consequences, but never reduce the game to an optimal setting guide, visible breeding chart, or deterministic recipe database.

## Law 17 — One active care space means one active organism

Each Hollow houses no more than one living Stem. Account maximum is three occupied Hollows.

## Law 18 — The Choosing cannot be undone

The Choosing commits a dominant adaptive direction. Regression does not reverse it, release does not preserve the living organism, and synthesis does not clone it.

## Law 19 — Network authority governs permanent change

All permanent, economic, mutation, entitlement, and release actions are server-authoritative. No queued offline mutation or economy operation is permitted.

## Law 20 — Launch scope is broad but bounded

The MVP includes cultivation, premium illustration, archive, synthesis, trading, gifting, subscription, and release. It excludes combat, direct social chat, currency markets, auctions, and speculative economy mechanics.
