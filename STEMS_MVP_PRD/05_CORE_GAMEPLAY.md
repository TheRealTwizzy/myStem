# Core Gameplay

## Core promise

The player can understand and influence a living biological process without directly designing it.

## Session loop

1. Enter a Hollow.
2. Observe posture, movement, rhythm, tissue behavior, environmental interaction, sound, and text.
3. Infer broad biological need.
4. Adjust one or more environmental controls.
5. Observe immediate response and later accumulated effect.
6. Resolve a Becoming when prompted.
7. Review the resulting Field Entry.
8. Sustain the changed anatomy.

## Account structure

Each player has exactly three Hollow slots.

- One Stem per Hollow.
- Zero to three living Stems total.
- Hollows cannot be purchased, merged, expanded, or converted into storage.
- A living Stem cannot move between accounts or Hollow slots after creation unless a future explicitly designed migration feature is approved.

## Starting a Stem

An empty Hollow offers:

### New Seed

Always available online.

### Lineage Synthesis

Available only when all synthesis requirements are valid.

The user first selects:

1. one of seven biome families;
2. one of that family's three sub-biomes.

The selected Hollow begins at:

- Temperate Warmth;
- Balanced Moisture;
- Low Light;
- unique variable band 3.

## Environmental interaction

Every occupied Hollow exposes four stepped controls.

Universal:

- Warmth
- Moisture
- Light

Unique:

- one sub-biome-specific variable

Each control has five labelled bands. The physical meaning of a label depends on sub-biome thresholds; the player sees the band, not the hidden numeric value.

Changes are server-authoritative and require online access.

## Meaningful observation

A meaningful observation is not a passive app open. It involves at least one of:

- inspecting a changed biological signal;
- responding to a need;
- adjusting the Hollow and receiving a response;
- maintaining an intervention;
- resolving a Becoming;
- handling a Field Entry;
- creating or releasing a Stem.

## Becoming readiness

A Becoming emerges from accumulated pressure and developmental readiness. The player is not shown a progress bar or probability.

Signals should escalate through:

- posture;
- rhythm;
- tissue activity;
- environment interaction;
- observational copy.

## Mutation Consent

### Stabilize [+]

The player actively maintains supportive conditions.

Requirements:

- explicit start;
- active online maintenance;
- supportive condition window;
- one Resume Support opportunity before commitment.

Results:

- narrower variability;
- coherent support;
- future sustainment requirements;
- minimal Mutation Debt in normal success.

### Suppress [-]

The player actively maintains opposing conditions.

Requirements:

- explicit start;
- active online maintenance;
- one uninterrupted attempt.

Results:

- visible growth may be reduced, redirected, or prevented;
- unresolved pressure may become Mutation Debt;
- cannot restart during the same Becoming.

### Let Continue [±]

The player neither actively supports nor opposes the change.

Results:

- broadest natural variability;
- full influence from current Hollow history, lineage, anatomy, and hidden debt;
- no implication of neglect.

Ordinary Hollow adjustment outside an active intervention never counts as hidden Stabilization or Suppression.

## Resolution

A resolved Becoming:

- commits anatomical changes;
- updates sustainment;
- may alter behavior and biofeedback;
- may advance stage;
- may resolve or redirect Mutation Debt;
- creates exactly one Field Entry;
- creates exactly one embedded Genetic Entry.

## Adult play

Settled Form is a long-term state, not an ending.

Adults can:

- continue to require care;
- experience slower Becomings;
- create more Field Entries;
- recover from strain;
- regress after prolonged unrecovered Decay;
- be released when eligible.

## Offline rule

Offline mode is read-only for biological and economic state. No queued meaningful changes.

## Failure philosophy

The player should experience consequence, not punishment.

- Poor sustainment creates readable strain.
- Prolonged mismatch can lead to regression.
- Ordinary care never permanently kills.
- Regression makes the remaining body easier to sustain.
- Release is voluntary and permanent.

## Tutorial requirements

The first standard Stemling tutorial must be viewed once.

Afterward the player may disable and re-enable tutorials.

Tutorial settings never disable:

- billing disclosures;
- irreversible-action confirmations;
- synthesis confirmations;
- trade confirmations;
- gift acceptance confirmation;
- release confirmation.
